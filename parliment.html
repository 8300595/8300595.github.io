Parliament House
What we do:

Parliament makes laws, authorises the Government to spend public money, scrutinises government activities, and is a forum for debate on national issues. Parliament House is the home of Australia's Parliament. The 226 members of parliament meet here to represent the Australian people and make decisions for the nation.
